Frequently Asked Questions

How does Upwork ensure quality for remote work?

Upwork offers several features to ensure quality: a transparent review and rating system, work history for freelancers, an optional "Expert-Vetted Talent" pool for top-tier professionals, and clear communication tools. We also provide resources on best practices for managing remote projects and telecommuting teams. Conceptual internal link: Read more about our trust and safety measures.

What are 'Connects' for freelancers, and how does the offer work?

Connects are virtual tokens freelancers use to submit proposals for projects. With our special offer, new freelancers receive 10 free Connects each month with their subscription, helping them apply to more "virtual work" opportunities without initial overhead. This is in addition to any Connects included with their chosen plan.

Is my payment secure when hiring a freelancer for a work from home project?

Yes, Upwork uses a secure payment system. For hourly projects, you're billed based on hours logged in the Work Diary. For fixed-price projects, you fund milestones, and payments are held in escrow and released only when you approve the work. This protects both clients and freelancers in "telecommuting" engagements.

Can I find talent for both short-term virtual tasks and long-term remote positions?

Absolutely! Upwork is flexible. You can find freelancers for quick, specific tasks (e.g., a few hours of "remote" data entry) or engage talent for ongoing, long-term remote roles (e.g., a part-time "virtual" marketing manager). Our platform supports various contract types to suit your needs.

What kind of support does Upwork offer for businesses new to managing hybrid or remote teams?

Upwork provides extensive resources, including articles, guides, and webinars on best practices for hiring and managing "remote" and "hybrid" teams. Our platform features tools designed to facilitate clear communication and project tracking for "virtual" collaborations. Plus, our customer support team is available to assist with any platform-related questions.
